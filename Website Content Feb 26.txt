## BUSINESS DESCRIPTION


### Core Business
SP Enterprises is specialized in precision components manufacture with advanced production technology and completed manufacture quality and management systems. The company makes products strictly according to customers' samples or drawings to manufacture several kinds of precision components and heavy earth moving spare parts.


### Business Evolution
The company started with precision components manufacturing and has expanded to become an importer and stockist for "GENUINE SPARES FOR HEAVY EARTHMOVING EQUIPMENT UNDER CARRIAGE PRODUCTS" under the name M/s SP Enterprises.


### Key Differentiators
- Rich experience in precision components
- Products range including the field of Heavy earth Grounded Engaging spare parts
- Excellent reputation in the industry
- Advanced production technology
- Complete quality management systems
- Custom manufacturing based on customer specifications

## INFRASTRUCTURE & MANUFACTURING CAPABILITIES


### State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Facility


The manufacturing facility is equipped with latest technology machines ensuring high precision and quality output.


#### 1. CNC TURNING CENTER
- **Purpose:** Better quality in terms of performance and finish
- **Capability:** High-precision turning operations
- **Advantage:** Consistent quality and excellent surface finish


#### 2. GEAR HOBBING MACHINES
- **Capability:** Manufacturing of Spur Gears and Helical Gears
- **Module Range:** 1 to 10 module
- **Applications:** Various gear manufacturing for heavy equipment


#### 3. CNC MACHINING CENTER
- **Specialty:** High accuracy in Hole Size and PCD (Pitch Circle Diameter)
- **Accuracy Level:** Less than 10 microns
- **Advantage:** Extreme precision for critical components


#### 4. GEAR SHAPING MACHINE
- **Purpose:** Produce internal Gears and Step Gears
- **Module Range:** 0.5 module to 6 module
- **Diameter Capacity:** Up to 600mm diameter
- **Advantage:** Complex gear geometries


#### 5. INDUCTION HARDENING MACHINE
- **Application:** Pins and Shafts heat treatment
- **Length Capacity:** Up to 2.5 meters in length
- **Advantage:** Enhanced durability and wear resistance


#### 6. GEAR GRINDING MACHINE
- **Purpose:** Grinding of Spur and Helical Gears
- **Module Range:** 1 to 6 modules
- **Advantage:** Superior surface finish and precision


### Quality Assurance
- Strong emphasis on quality across the entire product manufacturing cycle
- Use of high-end bi-metal bushes in swing Device planetary Gears
- Strict quality control at every stage
- Testing and validation procedures
